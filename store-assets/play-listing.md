## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
GPX Editor is a fast, offline toolkit for cyclists, runners and hikers who need to fix and reshape their GPS tracks. Merge two rides into one, trim dead time, repair broken elevation, convert between formats and shrink oversized files — all on your phone, with no account and no upload.

Your tracks never leave your device. Everything is processed locally, so it works on a plane, on a mountain, or anywhere with no signal.

▸ MERGE TRACKS
Combine multiple GPX or FIT files into one clean route. Smart handling of overlaps, time gaps and GPS teleports, with automatic segment splitting and an average-speed summary.

▸ TRIM & CLEAN
Cut off the warm-up, the drive home, or a forgotten stop. Drag the start and end handles and watch distance and time update live on the map.

▸ FIX ELEVATION
Repair noisy or spiky elevation and recompute total climb — ideal for barometric drift and bad GPS altitude.

▸ CONVERT FORMATS
Convert GPX to TCX or KML and back, and import FIT files from Garmin, Wahoo and other head units. Keep or strip heart rate, cadence and power.

▸ REDUCE FILE SIZE
Simplify dense tracks so they upload faster and stay under size limits — without ruining the route shape.

▸ COMPARE TRACKS
Overlay two recordings and compare power, heart rate or cadence, with time-shift alignment for dual-recording setups.

▸ MORE TOOLS
Reverse a route, strip and minify metadata, shift timestamps and remove pauses, plus one-tap "Repair file" cleanup for the most common GPS problems.

WHY GPX EDITOR
• Edits fully offline — your tracks are processed on-device and never uploaded
• No sign-up, no subscription
• Reads GPX and FIT; writes GPX, TCX and KML
• Interactive OpenStreetMap preview with pinch-zoom
• Metric or imperial units
• Clean, fast, and made for the trailhead

Great for anyone using Strava, Garmin Connect, Komoot, Wahoo or RideWithGPS who wants to tidy up a recording before uploading or sharing.

Free, supported by ads. No personal data is collected by us — see the privacy policy for details.
```

- **Privacy policy URL:** https://kubakunc.github.io/gpx-tooling/privacy-policy.html
  *(live after you enable GitHub Pages — see RELEASE.md)*
- **Data safety:** Your track files are processed on-device and not transmitted.
  But two Google SDKs DO collect data, so the Data-safety form must declare:
  - **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob):** Device or other IDs (advertising ID) — for
    Advertising/marketing. May be "shared" with Google.
  - **Firebase Analytics:** App activity / App interactions (usage events such as
    which tool is opened, share/save) and an analytics/advertising identifier — for
    Analytics. Collected.
  - Declare data is encrypted in transit; users cannot request deletion of these
    aggregated identifiers via the app (point them to Google's controls).
  Do NOT declare "no data collected" — that would be a false declaration.
